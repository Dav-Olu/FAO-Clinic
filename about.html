About FAO Dental Clinic

Welcome to FAO Dental Clinic, where your smile is our passion and your oral health is our priority. Located in a serene and accessible part of the city, FAO Dental Clinic is a modern, patient-centered dental facility committed to delivering high-quality,
affordable dental care in a comfortable and friendly environment.

Our team is made up of highly skilled and experienced dental professionals-dentists, hygienists, and support staff who are dedicated to excellence. Whether you're visiting us for a routine cleaning, cosmetic treatment, or a complex dental procedure, you can trust that you're in good hands.

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • General and Preventive Dentistry
  • Cosmetic Dentistry (Whitening, Veneers)
  • Orthodontics (Braces and Aligners)
  • Pediatric Dentistry
  • Tooth Replacement (Crowns, Bridges, Implants)
  • Emergency Dental Care

We understand that visiting the dentist can be stressful for some, which is why we've designed our clinic to be a calm and relaxing space. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll be greeted with a warm smile and treated with the utmost respect and care.

At FAO Dental Clinic, our General Preventive Dentistry services are designed to help patients maintain strong, healthy teeth and gums while preventing common dental issues before they arise. We focus on early detection and routine care as the foundation for long-term oral health. Our preventive approach includes regular dental chechups, professional cleanings, fluoride treatments, dental sealants, and patient education on proper brushing and flossing techniques.

At FAO Dental Clinic, our Cosmetic Dentistry services are tailored to enhance the beauty of your smile while improving your confidence and self-image. We understand that a bright, well-aligned smile cn make a powerful impression, and we are committed to helping you achieve the look you desire through safe, effective, and personalized cosmetic treatments.

At FAO Dental Clinic, our Pediatric Dentistry services are specially designed to provide gentle, comprehensive oral care for children from infancy through adolescence. We understand that early dental experiences can shape a child's attitude toward oral health for life, which is why we create a fun, friendly, and fear-free environment where young patients feel safe and comfortable.
